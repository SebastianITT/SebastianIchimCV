Second Year Project

For the second year project, I have created an online shop website.

The website had two types of users: administrator and customer. The administrator was able to add, update, delete, edit, access the items in the Admin toolbar, manage and update the stock and upload images.

The customer was able to access the website with a username and password. The customer had access to view the items, search for categories, add items to the shopping cart, manage the profile with address details, full name, email and phone number. I also had to create a list of requirements for administrator and customer, use case diagrams, use case descriptions, class diagrams and entity-relationship diagrams.
